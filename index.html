About Us

Welcome to Madina's Bakery! We are a family-owned bakery dedicated to providing our customers with the finest baked goods. Our journey began in 1990 when our founder, Madina, started baking delicious treats from her home kitchen. Today, we continue her legacy by offering a wide range of baked goods made with love and care.
